Darrel's Favorite Alfred Hitchcock Films

My top 3 film picks.

Rear Window

The first Hitchcock movie I ever saw. It is still one of my favorites and I watch it at least once a year. While not scary, you do wondering what will happen next and will love and justice prevail. I think James Stewart and Grace Kelly give their best on-screen performances.

Movie quote:

“I'd say she's doing a woman's hardest job: juggling wolves.”
- Lisa Fremont

To Catch A Thief

A great thriller and who-dun-it. Did John "The Cat" Robie, played by Cary Grant, return to a life of crime? Can Frances Stevens, played by Grace Kelly, fall in love with such a man? Watch the movie to find out.

Movie quote:

"You don't have to spend every day of your life proving your honesty, but I do"
- John Robie

The Birds

I believe this is the scariest Hitchcock movie. Different breeds of birds start attacking people. Why? Only the birds know and they aren't saying. Staring Rod Taylor and Tippy Hedren. Does Melanie, Tippy's character, play a part in the birds wrath or is she just in the wrong place at the wrong time. After watching this movie you'll want to put a padlock on Polly's cage and keep a fire going in the fireplace year round.

Movie quote:

"I'm neither poor nor innocent."
- Melanie Daniels
